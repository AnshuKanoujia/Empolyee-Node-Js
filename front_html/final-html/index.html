Features

Budget Prediction

Before making a final decision when selecting candidates to join your team, you will get a projected cost for each week as well as for duration of your project.

Time Tracking

Manually tracking time will be a thing of the past. Now technicians can clock in with the click of a button, and tech leads can view the hours in real time on a weekly basis.

Quality Assurance

Having responsible people on your team is important. We verify every employee by checking their driving, credit, and criminal history. Responsible candidates create a seamless and statistically safer work environment.

Intuitive Scheduling

See a list of available technicians, locally or nationally, based on your project 's start and end dates. Techs who aren 't available simply won 't appear in search results. This helps avoid overbooking and unnecessary phone and email communications.

Performance Rating

View a list of ratings for each technician based on jobs they have previously worked. We give you the power to know who you will be working with before making a commitment.

Location Tracking

Once you have selected a technician, you will receive a notification alerting you that they are on the way. With our ability to sync to a technician 's location, you can accurately know their current whereabouts and availability.

custom Staffing

With filtering options that allow you to select technicians based on skill level, performance ratings or distance, staffing your projects have never been more straight forward. PLM easily fits your needs in projecting costs, knowing the projected cost before you even hit "Submit."

Weekly Forecasts

Export files associated with actual cost projections and time-sheets after each work week has passed. We simplify valuable data transfer to view your project 's progress.
